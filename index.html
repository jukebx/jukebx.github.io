This site is material design. Google invented this design based off of paper, how it folds, and it's shadow.
Material Design (codenamed Quantum Paper) is a design language developed by Google. Expanding upon the "card" motifs that debuted in Google Now, Material Design makes more liberal use of grid-based layouts, responsive animations and transitions, padding, and depth effects such as lighting and shadows. Designer Mat√≠as Duarte explained that, "unlike real paper, our digital material can expand and reform intelligently. Material has physical surfaces and edges. Seams and shadows provide meaning about what you can touch." Google states that their new design language is based on paper and ink. Wikipedia

Contemporary design is a new style of design that challenges designers to the limits. By making one-page landing sites to mega-sites with over 20 pages, contemporary design is pretty cool. Body Evolve's new site that we created uses contemporary design. You can tell by the big buttons and spacious areas that it has been created using contemporary design.

We think that classical design is underated. Back in the early days of the web, classical design was all that was around. Now, we have so many other types that we sort of forget about classical design. Classical design is simple, easy to navigate, and simple. The cool thing about classical design is that you aren't limited to anything.

Zen pretty much speaks for itself. Zen design spaces things out. You just a sense of calmness by looking at your website with zen design. Zen design is popular for medical agencies, law firms, and many other things.
